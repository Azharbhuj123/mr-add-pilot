Background

Honest Home Hub started as a simple blog providing home improvement tips but quickly realized the need to connect their readers with trusted service providers. They approached us to build a comprehensive platform that could serve both content consumers and service seekers while creating a revenue-generating marketplace model.

Challenge & Objective

Honest Home Hub needed a comprehensive platform that could provide trustworthy home improvement advice while connecting homeowners with verified service vendors. Their existing website lacked the functionality to effectively match customers with the right professionals, resulting in low user engagement and minimal vendor sign-ups.

Our Solution

We built a custom WordPress and WooCommerce solution that combines an information-rich blog with a robust vendor marketplace. The platform features detailed product reviews, how-to guides, and a sophisticated vendor matching system that helps homeowners find the perfect service provider for their specific needs.

  • Custom WordPress theme optimized for content discovery

  • WooCommerce integration for vendor listings and bookings

  • Advanced search and filtering system for vendor matching

  • Review and rating system for both products and vendors

  • Shopify integration for product sales and inventory management

  • Mobile-responsive design with focus on user experience
