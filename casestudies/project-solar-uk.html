Background

As the UK government pushed renewable energy initiatives and energy costs soared, Project Solar UK saw massive market opportunity. However, they needed a digital presence that could educate potential customers, demonstrate clear ROI, and efficiently convert interest into qualified installation bookings at scale.

Challenge & Objective

Project Solar UK struggled to communicate the complex value proposition of solar installation to potential customers. Their website lacked the educational content and interactive tools needed to help customers understand savings, payback periods, and environmental impact. Lead quality was poor, and the sales team spent excessive time on unqualified inquiries.

Our Solution

We created an engaging, educational WordPress platform with custom solar savings calculators, interactive ROI visualizers, and comprehensive project galleries. The solution includes intelligent lead qualification forms, financing information tools, and automated consultation booking that ensures only qualified leads reach the sales team.

  • Educational content platform with solar savings calculators

  • Interactive ROI and payback period visualizer

  • Lead qualification system with financing eligibility checks

  • Project gallery with filterable case studies by property type

  • Automated consultation booking with team routing

  • Mobile-responsive design optimized for lead conversion
