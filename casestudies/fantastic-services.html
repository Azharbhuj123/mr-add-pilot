Background

Starting as a local cleaning service in London, Fantastic Services rapidly grew into a comprehensive home services marketplace. They needed technology infrastructure that could support their vision of becoming the leading international platform for connecting customers with trusted service professionals across dozens of service categories.

Challenge & Objective

Fantastic Services needed a scalable platform to manage their rapidly growing marketplace of service providers and customers. Their existing system couldn't handle the complexity of multi-service bookings, real-time scheduling, dynamic pricing, and service provider management. They were losing bookings due to poor mobile experience and lacked the infrastructure to expand internationally.

Our Solution

We built a sophisticated WordPress and WooCommerce marketplace platform with custom booking logic, intelligent service provider matching, real-time availability management, and multi-currency support. The solution includes automated scheduling, customer review systems, and comprehensive provider onboarding tools that enabled rapid market expansion.

  • Custom marketplace platform with WooCommerce integration

  • Intelligent service provider matching algorithm

  • Real-time availability and booking management system

  • Multi-currency and international market support

  • Automated scheduling with calendar synchronization

  • Review and rating system with quality assurance tools
